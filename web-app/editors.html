The Internet Politician Data Base

All citizens should have easy access to their representatives, even if the latter didn’t subscribe a Kuorum account.

Therefore we launched the Internet Politician Data Base (IPDB), an open data project to build the world’s largest dataset with up-to-date information about politicians.

Social and computational science researchers in top universities give support to this transparency wiki containing more than 4,000 politician profiles so far.
